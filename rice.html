USAGE INSTRUCTIONS

  1. Rinse the rice thoroughly before cooking to remove excess starch.
  2. Soak the rice in water for 30 minutes for optimal texture.
  3. Cook the rice using a ratio of 1 cup rice to 1.5 cups water.
  4. Bring to a boil, then simmer until the water is absorbed and the rice is tender.
  5. Fluff with a fork and let it sit for a few minutes before serving.
